A Band!

B Boys

Label: Captured Tracks

Members

  • Andrew Kerr, Drummer
  • Brendon Avalos, Singer/Gutiarist
  • Britton Walker, Gutiarist/Singer

B boys is a punk band based in New York. My friend Bobby (of Joan of Arc), introduced me to them in 2017

I have booked B Boys to play at State Street Pub here in Indianapolis, I've also seen them in Chicago and Toronto.

Bobby lives in Chicago, so I took a little trip to the city to see the Boys play at an outdoor block party in the middle of February called, "Music Frozen Dancing". It was a great show! Bobby has known B Boys a little long than I have, he invited all of us back to his place after.


B Boys at Music Frozen Dancing, Feb, 17, 2018
Check 'em out at their Bandcamp!

B Boys are on a small label, Captured Tracks.

They have two albums, 'Dada' and 'Dudu' and one EP 'No Worry No Mind' out. I can't wait to seem them back on road!
